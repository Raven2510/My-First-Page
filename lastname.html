Night Sky

After a long busy day of working with my school works, being exhausted and stressed about consecutive classes. I always find myself craving for something... It's not about food, but by going out for a while, taking some fresh air, and meditating to relax my head. Whenever I looked at the night sky, I was always captivated by its beauty, that I couldn't take my eyes off of it. The depth of darkness surrounded my eyes, the glowing moon, and the stars above. I always wonder how vast the space that contains billions of heavenly bodies is. That made me realize that my mind could never visualize the greatness of God through his creations.
